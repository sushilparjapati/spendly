Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any notes you provide.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and allowing you to manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely. We take reasonable precautions to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This results in a request to Google's servers when you visit the site. Beyond this, we do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond as promptly as possible.
